ABOUT ME

Antara Tarocchi

Tarot Advisor, Psychic Intuitive & Modern Mystic

From an early age, I’ve had the gift of bringing messages from beyond the veil. My connection to the Spirit World has always been strong, and as I grew, so did my yearning for deeper spiritual exploration. At just 13 years old, I got my hands on my first Tarot deck, and from that moment, my lifelong study of Tarot, Mysticism, and the Occult began—now spanning over 17 years.

With a solid foundation in ancient mystery traditions, my tarot readings blend my academic understanding of these esoteric teachings with my natural psychic abilities. For over 8 years, I have been reading the cards professionally for a worldwide audience, sharing mystical wisdom and psychic insights to unveil hidden messages and help manifest radiant, fulfilling lives. My goal is to guide clients in transforming and growing as we explore the mysteries of the unknown together.

Born in America, I now live as a modern-day nomad, traveling the world and learning from various spiritual traditions. Alongside my intuitive gifts, I have studied European Ritual Magick, Egyptian Spirituality, Eastern Mysticism, and the Energy Healing Arts. I am also a Certified Tarot Reader in the Cabot Tradition.

When I’m not working within the mystical realms, I enjoy the arts, silversmithing, gardening, connecting with nature, and spending time with my beloved dog, Pip Pip.

Tarot Readings

Tarot Card Readings involve the use of a deck of 78 cards, each adorned with symbolic images and mystical meanings. These images act as a tool for psychic reflection, allowing me to tap into spiritual insights and guide clients with the messages I receive. My tarot readings blend deep academic knowledge of the cards’ symbolism with my natural psychic abilities, offering a unique and comprehensive experience.

Tarot readings are a powerful form of divination that offer intuitive guidance and deep spiritual reflection. They provide a lens into the hidden facets of your life, allowing you to understand the deeper meanings behind the circumstances you face.

During a session, I will shuffle and lay out the Tarot cards, using my connection with them to interpret messages and provide insights into your situation. Whether you come with specific questions or prefer an open reading, the Tarot can reveal answers to any area of life—be it love, career, or your spiritual journey.

As a practitioner deeply rooted in intuitive wisdom and with the unique energy of six planets in Scorpio, I am naturally attuned to uncovering hidden motives, energies, and the secretive nature of those around you. My readings are designed to offer clarity, uplift your spirit, and help you make decisions with a new understanding of your circumstances. I pride myself on offering judgment-free and 100% confidential readings. No topic is too big or too small, and my readings are open to all—welcoming BIPOC, LGBTQ+, non-monogamous/ polyamorous individuals, and people from all walks of life. I offer guidance with an open heart and mind, meeting you exactly where you are.

How does Antara prepare for my reading ?

Before your reading take a few moments to ground and collect your energy, relax and prepare yourself. Feel free to think of some questions or a general topic you would like to look into. If you want a general reading that's perfectly fine. Just be sure to let me know either way. Have a glass of water and a notepad if you would like to take notes.
